About Us

Jonesboro Ridge Rider Mountain Bike Team

Our Story

The Jonesboro Ridge Rider Mountain Bike Team was established in 2020. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing middle school and high school athletes with the opportunity to experience the excitement and camaraderie of mountain biking.

Who We Are

We welcome new athletes starting in 5th grade. No matter the skill level, our team is for everyone. Whether you're an experienced rider or new to the sport, we strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment where every athlete can thrive.

What We Do

Our primary competition is in the Arkansas NICA League, where we participate in events that challenge our skills and bring us together as a team. Our pre-season begins in April, and we start regular practices in July, focusing on both personal growth and team success.

Our Philosophy

While we are a race team, our focus goes beyond race results. Our goal is constant improvement, building confidence, and fostering a love for mountain biking. We have a team of dedicated coaches who work tirelessly to ensure every athlete has the greatest experience possible.

Community Involvement

Giving back to the community is a core value of our team. We actively contribute through trail work, volunteer services, and advocacy for the biking community. Whether it's' maintaining local trails or participating in events that promote biking, we aim to leave a positive impact and inspire others to join the cycling movement.
