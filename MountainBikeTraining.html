Mountain Bike Training Plan

Week 1

Day Plan
Monday Recovery Ride: 60 min in Z1 (flat terrain).
Tuesday Warm-up: 15 min.
Intervals: 5 x 3 min @ Z5 (VO2 Max) with 3 min rest.
Cool down: 10 min.
Wednesday Skills session: Cornering, line choice, and technical descents (90 min).
Thursday Tempo ride: 90 min in Z3. Include 3 x 10 min efforts @ 85% FTP.
Friday Strength training:
- Squats: 4x8 reps.
- Deadlifts: 3x10 reps.
- Core: Planks (3 x 60 sec).
Saturday Long Endurance Ride: 3-4 hours in Z2. Include 30 minutes of climbing practice.
Sunday Trail ride: 2 hours focusing on flow and maintaining pace on technical terrain.

Week 2

Day Plan
Monday Active recovery: 60 min, flat ride in Z1.
Tuesday Intervals: 6 x 3 min @ Z5 with 3 min rest.
Wednesday Skills session: Bunny hops, steep climbs, and rock garden practice (90 min).
Thursday Sweet spot ride: 2 hours in Z3-Z4. Include 4 x 15 min efforts @ 90% FTP.
Friday Core/strength training (similar to Week 1).
Saturday Endurance Ride: 3.5 hours in Z2, with 4 x 10 min climbs at Z4.
Sunday MTB Race Simulation: 3 x 20 min at race pace (technical loop).
